Ingredients:

* 1 pound flank steak, sliced against the grain
* 1/2 cup peanut oil
* 1/4 cup soy sauce
* 1/4 cup white vinegar
* 1/4 cup honey
* 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
* 1 teaspoon garlic powder
* 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
* 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
* 1/2 cup chopped red onion
* 1/2 cup chopped celery
* 1/2 cup chopped carrots
* 1/2 cup roasted peanuts

Instructions:

1. In a large bowl, whisk together the peanut oil, soy sauce, vinegar, honey, Dijon mustard, garlic powder, and black pepper.
2. Add the steak strips to the bowl and toss to coat.
3. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or up to overnight.
4. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
5. Remove the steak strips from the marinade and grill for 4-5 minutes per side, or until cooked to your desired doneness.
6. Transfer the steak strips to a cutting board and let rest for a few minutes before slicing.
7. In a large bowl, combine the lettuce, red onion, celery, carrots, and peanuts.
8. Drizzle with the marinade from the steak and toss to coat.
9. Serve the salad topped with the sliced steak.
